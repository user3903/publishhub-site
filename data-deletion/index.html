Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 2026

PublishHub is a fully local desktop application. We do not operate a cloud backend and we do not store any user data on external servers.

What Data Exists

All data used by the App is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Local settings and preferences
  • Generated videos
  • Temporary files created during video generation
  • OAuth tokens for connected platforms (e.g., TikTok) — only if you choose to connect them

Because we do not store data remotely, all data deletion is performed directly on your device.

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Remove connected accounts inside the App (if applicable).
  2. Delete the App’s local data folder (optional). This folder contains generated videos and settings.
  3. Uninstall the App from your device.

After uninstalling, no data related to PublishHub remains on your device.

No User Accounts

PublishHub does not require or provide user accounts. There is no online profile, no cloud storage, and no server‑side data to delete.

Need Help?
