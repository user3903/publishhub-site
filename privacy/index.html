Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20th 2025

PublishHub (“we”, “our”, “the App”) is a desktop application that allows users to connect their social media accounts and publish content. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you explicitly authorize through social media login flows:

  • OAuth tokens (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
  • Basic profile information provided by each platform
  • Content you choose to upload through the App

We do NOT collect passwords, payment information, analytics, or personal files outside the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to authenticate your accounts, publish content, manage scheduled posts, and display your profile and media inside the App.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All OAuth tokens and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a cloud backend.

4. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you remove an account, uninstall the App, or request deletion.

5. Third‑Party Services

The App interacts with Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. Their policies apply to your use of their services.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, email: contact@publishhub.online
